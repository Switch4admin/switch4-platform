Our Story

Built on Relationships. Driven by Results.

Founded and incorporated in Newark, Delaware, Switch4 LLC was built on a singular belief: that recruitment done right changes lives and transforms organizations. We are strategists, advocates, and talent architects — not just headhunters.

We combine a genuine curiosity about people with deep market expertise across 15+ industries. Our consultants take the time to understand not just the role, but the company, the culture, and the human being who will thrive there.

Our mission is clear: to connect exceptional talent with forward-thinking organizations through excellence, accountability, and transparency — delivering the right people who drive lasting success.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We hold ourselves and every engagement to the highest standard. Rigorous vetting, precise matching, and an uncompromising commitment to quality — in every single search.

Accountability

We take full ownership of outcomes. If a placement doesn't work, we don't walk away — we make it right. Your success is the only measure that matters to us.

Transparency

Clear communication at every stage. No hidden fees, no spin, no surprises. You'll always know where your search stands and why every decision was made.
