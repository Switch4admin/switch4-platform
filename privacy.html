Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025  ·  Switch4 LLC

Switch4 LLC ("Switch4", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit www.switch4.co, use our AI chatbot, or submit your information through our forms or recruitment process.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Professional information: Resume, job history, skills, certifications
  • Application details: Roles you're interested in, availability, work authorization status
  • Communications: Messages sent via our contact form or AI chatbot

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser local storage (used for chat history — stored only on your device)

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or behavioral advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with relevant job opportunities and present your profile to potential employers
  • To respond to your inquiries via contact form, email, or phone
  • To create and manage your candidate profile in our Applicant Tracking System (JobDiva)
  • To send you relevant job alerts and recruiter communications (with your consent)
  • To improve our website, chatbot performance, and recruitment services
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With client employers: When you apply for a specific role or give consent for your profile to be presented
  • Service providers: JobDiva (ATS), email delivery services, cloud infrastructure — all under data processing agreements
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with appropriate notice

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

4. AI Chatbot (Ava)

Our AI chatbot, Ava, is powered by a third-party Large Language Model (LLM) accessed via a secure backend proxy. When you use the chatbot:

  • Your messages are sent to our server and forwarded to the LLM provider for processing
  • The LLM provider processes your messages per their own privacy policy
  • We do not store raw LLM conversations on our servers beyond the session
  • Chat history is stored only in your browser's local storage until you clear it or request deletion
  • Personal data you provide (name, email, resume) is handled as described in this policy

Ava may make mistakes. Do not share sensitive information such as Social Security numbers, financial account details, or passwords in the chat.

5. Data Retention

  • Candidate profiles: Retained for up to 3 years from last activity, then deleted or anonymized
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for 2 years
  • Browser local storage (chat history): Controlled by you — clear at any time via the chatbot's "Delete My Data" button or your browser settings
  • Uploaded resumes: Retained as long as your candidate profile is active

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request erasure of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@switch4.co with subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

For local browser data deletion, use the "Delete My Data" button in the chatbot, or clear your browser's local storage manually.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure backend proxies — no API keys or credentials exposed in browser JavaScript
  • Input sanitization and XSS protection on all forms
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints
  • Access controls limiting who can access candidate data
  • Security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options)

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a breach affecting your rights, we will notify affected individuals as required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g., LinkedIn, employer websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
